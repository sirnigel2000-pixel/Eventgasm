## Accelerator #2: Affiliate Optimization ($6K/month)

### Current: 5-10% commission
### Target: 800 ticket sales/month × $75 avg = $6,000

### How to Increase Conversions

1. **"Buy Tickets" button prominence**
   - Make it the primary CTA
   - Add to event cards (not just detail page)
   - Sticky button on scroll

2. **Price comparison**
   - Show prices from multiple sources
   - "Cheapest on SeatGeek" badge
   - Users click more when they see savings

3. **Urgency triggers**
   - "Only 12 tickets left"
   - "Price went up $5 since yesterday"
   - "23 people viewing now"

4. **Retargeting**
   - Push notification: "Tickets for [event you viewed] are selling fast"
   - Email: "Events you saved are coming up"

### Affiliate Mix

| Partner | Commission | Focus |
|---------|------------|-------|
| SeatGeek | 6-10% | Concerts, sports |
| StubHub | 8-12% | Premium/resale |
| Ticketmaster | 5-8% | Major tours |
| VividSeats | 10-15% | Best commission |
| Eventbrite | 5% | Local events |

## Accelerator #3: Ad Revenue Boost ($3.5K/month)

### Target: 350,000 monthly ad impressions @ $10 CPM

### Ad Placement Strategy

| Placement | Impressions/User | CPM |
|-----------|------------------|-----|
| Home feed (native) | 3 | $8 |
| Between events | 5 | $6 |
| Event detail banner | 2 | $10 |
| Map view | 1 | $5 |
| Search results | 2 | $12 |

### Premium Ad Tiers (Higher CPM)
- **Location-based ads** - Venues advertising to nearby users ($15-25 CPM)
- **Event category ads** - Spotify on music events ($20-30 CPM)
- **Sponsored events** - Pay to appear in feed ($50-100 per placement)

### User Scale Needed
- 350K impressions ÷ 10 impressions/user = 35,000 MAU
- Target: 50,000 users by Month 12

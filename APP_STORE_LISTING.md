## Description (4000 characters max)
```
Stop searching. Start experiencing.

Eventgasm brings together events from everywhere — concerts, sports, theater, comedy, festivals, and free local happenings — all in one beautiful app. No more jumping between Ticketmaster, Eventbrite, and a dozen other sites.

🎵 WHAT YOU'LL FIND
• Concerts & live music from your favorite artists
• Sports games — NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, college & more
• Theater, Broadway tours & performing arts
• Comedy shows & stand-up
• Family-friendly events & festivals
• FREE local events — library programs, parks & rec, community gatherings

📍 LOCATION-AWARE
• Shows events near you automatically
• Search any city in the US
• Set your radius — 5 miles to 50 miles

🎟️ COMPARE TICKET OPTIONS
• See prices from Ticketmaster, SeatGeek, Eventbrite
• One-tap access to StubHub & VividSeats for resale tickets
• Find the best deal without opening 5 different apps

📅 SMART SHOWTIMES
• Events with multiple dates are grouped together
• Pick your preferred date/time
• Never miss a show because you didn't know about the matinee

🆓 FREE EVENTS SPOTLIGHT
• Dedicated section for free happenings
• Library events, outdoor movies, community festivals
• Because the best things in life are free

WHY EVENTGASM?
We built this because finding events shouldn't be a research project. Whether you're planning a date night, looking for weekend plans, or just wondering "what's happening near me?" — Eventgasm gives you the answer in seconds.

No account required. No spam. Just events.

Download now and never miss another moment. 🎉
```

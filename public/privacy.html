🎉 Eventgasm Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

Welcome to Eventgasm! We're committed to protecting your privacy while helping you discover amazing events. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

The Short Version: We collect location data to show you nearby events, and optional account info if you sign up. We don't sell your personal data. You can delete your data anytime.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Show relevant events: Your location helps us display events happening near you.
  • Personalize recommendations: Your favorites and browsing history improve our suggestions.
  • Send notifications: Event reminders and updates (with your permission).
  • Improve our service: Analytics help us fix bugs and build better features.
  • Customer support: Respond to your questions and feedback.

3. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Companies that help us operate (hosting, analytics, push notifications). They're contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Advertising partners: We may share anonymized, aggregated data with ad networks to show relevant ads. This data cannot identify you personally.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Retention

  • Account data: Kept until you delete your account.
  • Favorites: Kept until you remove them or delete your account.
  • Location data: Not stored permanently; used only for current session.
  • Analytics: Aggregated data retained for up to 24 months.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: Request a copy of data we have about you.
  • Delete your data: Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Opt out of location: Deny or revoke location permission anytime.
  • Opt out of notifications: Disable push notifications in device settings.
  • Opt out of personalized ads: Use your device's "Limit Ad Tracking" setting.

How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your account and all data by:

  • Going to Profile → Settings → Delete Account in the app
  • Emailing us at privacy@eventgasm.com

6. Data Security

We protect your information using:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with regular security updates
  • Limited employee access to personal data
  • Regular security assessments

While we strive to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your device secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Eventgasm is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@eventgasm.com.

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:

  • Right to Know: Request what personal information we've collected.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information.
  • Non-Discrimination: We won't discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

9. International Users

Eventgasm is operated in the United States. If you're accessing from outside the US, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from your country.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for material changes

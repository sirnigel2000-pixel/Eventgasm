🎉 Eventgasm Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Eventgasm ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

Information We Collect

Location Data: With your permission, we collect your device's location to show you nearby events. This data is used only to provide location-based services and is not stored on our servers.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app, such as which features are most popular. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone) unless you contact us
  • Payment information (all ticket purchases happen on third-party sites)
  • Social media data
  • Contacts or photos

How We Use Your Information

  • To show you events near your location
  • To improve our app and services
  • To respond to your inquiries if you contact us

Third-Party Services

Our app displays events from third-party sources including Ticketmaster, SeatGeek, Eventbrite, StubHub, and VividSeats. When you click to buy tickets, you will be redirected to these third-party websites, which have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies before making purchases.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
